# Dashboard-Sales-Tableau

This interactive Tableau dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance, profitability, and product demand across multiple regions within the United States. Designed for executives, sales managers, and business analysts, the dashboard consolidates key performance indicators (KPIs) into a single view, enabling stakeholders to monitor business performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions efficiently.

## Overview

The top section highlights three primary KPIs: **Total Sales ($733K)**, **Total Profit ($93K)**, and **Total Quantity Sold (#12K)**. Each KPI is accompanied by trend lines that compare current performance against previous periods, allowing users to quickly assess growth patterns, seasonality, and overall business momentum. Percentage growth indicators provide immediate insight into year-over-year performance changes, helping decision-makers evaluate whether strategic objectives are being achieved.

A geographic visualization displays the **Sales and Profit Distribution by State**, offering a detailed view of business performance across the United States. Through size and color variations, users can identify high-performing and underperforming states, uncover regional opportunities, and understand where sales activities generate the greatest returns. This geographic analysis supports territory management, resource allocation, and regional sales planning.

The dashboard also includes a **Monthly Sales by Segment** analysis, breaking down performance across key customer segments such as Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office. These visualizations reveal monthly sales fluctuations and segment-specific purchasing behaviors, enabling organizations to identify their most valuable customer groups and tailor marketing or sales strategies accordingly.

At the bottom-left section, comparative dot plots categorize states as performing above or below the national average for both sales and profit. These insights help managers pinpoint regions requiring attention, replicate successful practices from top-performing areas, and optimize business operations to improve overall profitability.

The bottom-right section presents **Total Sales by Location and Manager**, ranking sales contributions across regions and responsible managers. This view enables performance benchmarking, facilitates accountability, and supports management decisions related to sales targets, incentives, and workforce planning. Regional sales totals provide additional context regarding market contribution and geographic business strength.

Overall, this dashboard serves as a powerful business intelligence solution that transforms raw sales data into actionable insights. By integrating KPI monitoring, geographic analysis, customer segmentation, profitability assessment, and managerial performance tracking, the dashboard empowers organizations to improve operational efficiency, maximize revenue opportunities, and support strategic growth initiatives through data-driven decision-making.
